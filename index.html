What is vyvn [vahy-vin]?

Vyvn is a Burning Man camp of passionate artists, builders and creators. We strive to create a lively and welcoming vibe in Black Rock City, NYC or wherever we may be.

We celebrated our first year on the playa at 3:15 & G during Burning Man 2015. Thank you to everyone who came out to enjoy some great music and bubbly with us. If you weren't able to make it out there, keep an eye out for our events in NYC throughout the year.
