What is vyvn [vahy-vin]?

Vyvn is an up-and-coming Burning Man camp full of passionate artists, builders and creators. We strive to create a lively and welcoming vibe wherever we may be, whether Burning Man or the default world.

We celebrated our first year on the playa this year at 3:15 & G. Thank you to everyone who came out to enjoy some great music and bubbly with us. If you weren't able to make it out there, keep an eye out for our events in NYC throughout the year.
